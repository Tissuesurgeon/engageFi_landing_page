The Problem (With Real Numbers)

The digital attention economy is fundamentally broken, creating inefficiency, inequity, and a growing reliance on fake engagement practices.

Broken Content Discovery

Global digital advertising exceeds $600 billion annually, yet organic reach on platforms like Instagram and Facebook has fallen below 5% for most creators. Average CPM costs range from $6-$18 across major platforms, pricing out small creators.

Facebook/Instagram: $7-$14 CPM
YouTube: $9-$18 CPM
X (Twitter): $6-$10 CPM

Exploited User Attention

Users spend 2.5+ hours per day on social media and over 900 hours per year generating engagement. Platforms monetize this attention through advertising and data extraction, yet users earn $0 for their participation.

Average user generates $30-$50/year in ad revenue
With 4.5+ billion users: $135-$225 billion captured annually

The Fake Engagement Economy

Rising ad costs have created a parallel gray market estimated at $1-$1.5 billion annually in fake likes, bot comments, and engagement farms. This distorts analytics, violates platform policies, and erodes trust.

Fake likes, bot comments, engagement farms
Creators face a dilemma: pay high costs or use unethical alternatives

The TrueEngage Solution

A transparent, blockchain-enabled marketplace for attention where creators pay for verified human engagement and users are fairly rewarded for meaningful participation.

Core Principle: Attention is a form of labor — and labor should be compensated.

TrueEngage operates as a neutral engagement settlement layer, aligning incentives between creators and users while eliminating bots, fake traffic, and misleading engagement metrics.

For Creators & Brands

Submit content and define engagement tasks such as watching content, providing feedback, writing meaningful comments, or voting. Allocate a fixed engagement budget and receive measurable human interaction.

Example: Creator allocates 100 TRUE tokens → Exactly 100 verified users complete the task → No overspend, no fake traffic

For Users

Browse available engagement tasks, complete them honestly, submit proof of engagement, and earn rewards for participation. For users in emerging markets, even modest rewards ($1-$3 per task) can represent meaningful income.

Key Benefit: Your attention has value. Get paid fairly for meaningful participation.

Transparent & Secure

Payments are locked and released via escrow. Engagement quality is verified and prioritized over raw volume. Blockchain is used strictly as infrastructure, not speculation.

Guarantee: No bots. No fake traffic. No opaque pricing. Complete transparency.
